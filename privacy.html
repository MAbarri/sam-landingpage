Privacy Policy for SAM - Smart Assistant Mate

Introduction

HODHOD LTD ("we" or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and disclose personal information through our mobile app, SAM - Smart Assistant Mate ("the app"). By using the app, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy.

Personal Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide to us when you use the app, including your name, email address, and device information.
